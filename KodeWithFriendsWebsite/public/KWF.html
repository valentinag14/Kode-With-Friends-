Kode With Friends


Kode With Friends (K.W.F) is a program that introduces middle and high school students to the fundamentals of coding. Participants will get to create their projects through mentorships, interactive lessons, and workshops. Participants will explore popular programming languages like Python, HTML, JavaScript, Java, SQL, and Snap Berkeley. This program offers a supportive environment for Participants who aspire to be software developers or are interested in coding and building a strong foundation for future success in the STEM field.
